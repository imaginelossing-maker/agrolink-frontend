Farmer Terms & Conditions

Last updated: January 2024

how_to_reg 1. Eligibility & Registration

  • Must be at least 18 years old to register as a seller on AgroLink.
  • Must provide valid identification documents for verification.
  • Must own or have legal rights to sell the agricultural products listed.
  • Must have a valid bank account for receiving payments.
  • Registration requires completion of all mandatory profile information.

verified 2. Verification & Tier System

  • All farmers must complete basic verification before listing products.
  • Basic verification includes ID, address, and farm location verification.
  • Advanced tiers require additional documentation and farm inspections.
  • Higher tiers provide increased visibility and transaction limits.
  • Verification status must be kept current and updated annually.

inventory_2 3. Product Listing Requirements

  • All products must be accurately described with real, recent photos.
  • Product specifications must include quantity, quality grade, and harvest date.
  • Prices must be competitive and reflect current market conditions.
  • Prohibited from listing products you don't own or cannot deliver.
  • Must maintain accurate inventory levels and update listings promptly.

local_shipping 4. Delivery & Fulfillment

  • Must ship orders within the agreed timeframe (typically 3-5 business days).
  • Must use reliable shipping services with tracking capabilities.
  • Responsible for proper packaging to ensure product quality during transit.
  • Must communicate any delays to the buyer through AgroLink messaging.
  • Delivery confirmation required before payment release from escrow.

payments 5. Payments & Fees

  • AgroLink charges a commission fee on each successful transaction.
  • Commission rates vary based on seller tier and product category.
  • Payments are released from escrow upon buyer confirmation of delivery.
  • Withdrawal requests are processed within 5-7 business days.
  • Transaction fees are deducted before payment to seller's bank account.

star 6. Quality Standards

  • Must meet or exceed advertised quality specifications.
  • Products must be fresh, properly stored, and free from contamination.
  • Must comply with Nigerian agricultural standards and regulations.
  • Quality complaints may result in account review and potential downgrade.
  • Repeated quality violations may lead to account suspension.

support_agent 7. Customer Service

  • Must respond to buyer inquiries within 24 hours.
  • Must maintain professional communication at all times.
  • Must use AgroLink's messaging system for all buyer communications.
  • Must cooperate with dispute resolution processes.
  • Must provide accurate and helpful information about products.

warning 8. Prohibited Activities

  • Listing counterfeit or misrepresented products.
  • Manipulating prices, ratings, or transaction history.
  • Offering to transact outside AgroLink platform.
  • Harassing or abusing buyers or other sellers.
  • Using multiple accounts to bypass restrictions.
  • Violating any Nigerian agricultural or trade laws.

gavel 9. Account Suspension & Termination

  • AgroLink reserves the right to suspend accounts for policy violations.
  • Minor violations may result in temporary suspension or warnings.
  • Severe violations will result in permanent account termination.
  • Suspended accounts cannot list new products or receive payments.
  • Terminated accounts forfeit any pending payments or commissions.

policy 10. Liability & Indemnification

  • AgroLink is not liable for product quality or delivery issues.
  • Sellers are responsible for their own product quality and fulfillment.
  • Sellers agree to indemnify AgroLink from claims related to their products.
  • AgroLink's liability is limited to the commission fees paid.
  • Force majeure events may excuse temporary non-performance.

By registering as a seller on AgroLink Nigeria, you agree to these terms and conditions. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of any updates to these terms.
