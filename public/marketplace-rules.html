Marketplace Rules

Last updated: January 2024

verified_user 1. Account Registration & Verification

  • All users must provide accurate and complete information during registration.
  • Sellers (farmers) must complete verification before listing products for sale.
  • Each user is allowed only one account per role (buyer or seller).
  • You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials.

shopping_cart 2. Product Listings

  • All products must be accurately described with real photos and specifications.
  • Sellers must list only agricultural products they own or have rights to sell.
  • Prohibited items include illegal substances, counterfeit goods, and non-agricultural products.
  • Prices must be fair and reflect the quality and quantity of the product.

payments 3. Transactions & Payments

  • All transactions are protected by our escrow system until delivery is confirmed.
  • Payment must be made through AgroLink's secure payment system only.
  • External payment arrangements are prohibited and may result in account suspension.
  • Refunds are processed according to our dispute resolution policy.

local_shipping 4. Delivery & Logistics

  • Sellers must ship orders within the agreed timeframe.
  • Buyers must confirm receipt within 7 days of delivery.
  • Both parties must communicate through AgroLink's messaging system.
  • Delivery disputes will be resolved based on provided evidence and tracking information.

gavel 5. Prohibited Activities

  • False advertising or misrepresentation of products.
  • Manipulating ratings, reviews, or transaction history.
  • Harassment, abuse, or inappropriate behavior toward other users.
  • Attempting to bypass AgroLink's fees or payment systems.
  • Using the platform for fraudulent or illegal activities.

report_problem 6. Violations & Penalties

  • Minor violations may result in warnings or temporary account suspension.
  • Severe or repeated violations will lead to permanent account termination.
  • AgroLink reserves the right to remove listings that violate these rules without notice.
  • Legal action may be taken for fraudulent activities.

support_agent 7. Dispute Resolution

  • Disputes should first be resolved through direct communication between parties.
  • AgroLink provides mediation services for unresolved disputes.
  • Final decisions on disputes rest with AgroLink's administration.
  • Both parties agree to abide by AgroLink's dispute resolution process.

By using AgroLink Nigeria, you agree to abide by these marketplace rules. Violations may result in account suspension or termination.
